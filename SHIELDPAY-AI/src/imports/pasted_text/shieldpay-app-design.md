Design a professional mobile app UI prototype for a fintech insurance application called **ShieldPay – AI Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Workers**.

The app provides weekly parametric insurance for gig delivery workers. Workers from platforms like Zomato, Swiggy, Amazon, Zepto, and Dunzo can register using their worker ID. The app verifies their platform, tracks their orders, checks their location, and uses AI to calculate income loss caused by external conditions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, floods, or high air pollution.
